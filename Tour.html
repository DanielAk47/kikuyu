Ceremonies ,the beauty do's and don'ts

ceremonies

The Kikuyus have numerous ceremonies such as birth, naming, circumcision, marriage, cleansing and death respectively. The ceremonies are all celebrated with joy except for death ceremony and they believed that these ceremonies symbolized the rights of passage.These  ceremonies are celebrated during different times of the year and with  a reason . These ceremonies were being lead by the council of elders where the chief of the village or the clan  leader could always be incharge.

Do's and do'nts


Show respect to the elders 
Inquire more about the culture
Respect the cultural believe and follow them
Respect other peoples opinion

Do'nts
Do not assume anyone is like you
Never argue with the elders
Never  disrespect  anyones idea
