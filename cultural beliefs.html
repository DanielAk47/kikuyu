 CULTURAL BELIEFS


The Agikuyu believe in a supreme being (Ngai), who lives on Mount Kenya. The name 'Ngai', means ‘one creator God’,to whom they offer prayers and sacrifices at designated places, such as on top of hills, Mount Kenya and under specific trees. Sacrifices are conducted by members of the ruling generation, who appoint special priests for each occasion. They also belief that Ngai is the one who created their first parents , Gikuyu and Mumbi. They were living at mukuruwe  wa nyagathanga where kikuyu community people believe it is the sacred place.
The gikuyu people used to praise and worship their creator under a tree whicch they  believed to be sacred and holy. The tree they called it mugumo ( the fig tree).
